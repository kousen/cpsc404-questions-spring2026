## Week 08 – Dual Reading Question  
**Shazif Ahmed**

In *Help Your Boss Help You*, Chapter 4 emphasizes proactively sending the “Important Two Messages” to reduce ambiguity and build trust through clear upward communication. In *Producing Open Source Software*, Chapter 8 discusses managing participants in decentralized communities where authority is often informal and contributors are volunteers.

How do the “Important Two Messages” translate to open source leadership, where contributors may not have a traditional “boss”? Can  clarifying expectations and progress function as a substitute for formal hierarchy in open source communities?
